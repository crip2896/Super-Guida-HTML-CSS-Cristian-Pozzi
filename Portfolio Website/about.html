About me.

from pastry chef to UX/UI Designer student, this is my story...

My story...

I want to tell you about myself, how I wanted to change direction and how I am trying to find a different future

I come from beautiful Italy, precisely from Aprilia in the Lazio region. I studied at the hotel school and immediately after finishing school i started working in pastry. I liked the job but I felt that something was missing ... After about five years of this profession, despite my constant commitment, no one has ever offered me a fixed contract; until the covid 19 pandemic began and the last employers, rightly, did not renew my contract ... and I found myself at home not knowing what I would do in the future and if there was a future.

At that moment i felt more and more alive the feeling that something was missing and it is thanks to that feeling that i felt the need to look elsewhere... Then i started to find out about the professionals who went around the web. And i arrived at the UX / UI that caught my attention right away, seeing myself so interested I decided to invest time and money to study this profession.

And here I am trying to change my future!
