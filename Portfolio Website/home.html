My skills...



Adobe illustrator

I love this program because it allows me to blend shapes and play with them. With illustrator I can produce excellent presentations or different brands and logos thanks to the variety of tools available; these tools help me to create always different products to satisfy customers.

Photoshop

It is very useful as an aid to integrate images to all my projects. with photoshop I can best crop and edit all the images I need. Photoshop and illustrator are used simultaneously, and this is very important for a quality work.

Web design

I really like playing with the imagination and making the best shapes to adapt and position them in the best way on a web page. The web is a beautiful place and simplicity is the best way to go.

Logo design

this is the part that I like the most about my job, I love creating the most varied logos according to the client. The logo is the business card of every company and must perfectly reflect the character of the company, in short it must immediately communicate who and what we are.

Typography

Typography is one of the most important parts of our job, with it we can communicate to users who we are what we do and why and it is fundamental. For this reason, typography should not be ignored.
